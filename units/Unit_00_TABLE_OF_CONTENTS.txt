PREFACE
This book titled “CONTEMPORARY INDIA AND EDUCATION” has
been written based on the new syllabus of the B.Ed. course of
the Tamilnadu Teacher Education University introduced in
2021. The five units of this subject has been discussed in detail
under the following headings :

Unit! | Education in Contemporary India and Constitutional

To facilitate the easy understanding of the subject
by the teacher trainees, the contents have been
presented under different headings and sub-headings.
As of our previous works, this book is also written in a
lucid style with vivid explanations and apt examples. To
help students prepare for their examinations, review
questions and the expected answers for the same have
been provided at, the end of each lesson. A set of
University Model Question Papers with the expected
answers for the same cited in the book, have been
supplemented with this book to help students who take
their B.Ed. Degree Examination under the new syllabus.
